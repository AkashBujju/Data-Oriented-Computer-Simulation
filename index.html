  • Features and Functionality

    • Vehicles
      • Cars(All cars look the same, with different colors)
      • Trucks(All trucks look the same, with different colors)
      • Busses(All busses look the same, with different colors)
    • Buildings
      • Homes(small, medium, large)(small, medium, large homes will differ visually, with different colors)
      • Factories(small, medium, large)(small, medium, large factories will differ visually, with different colors)
      • Garbage(landfill, refinary)(landfill and refinary have different visuals, but have a fixed color)
      • PowerGenerators(windmill, coal, oil, wiring)(all of them look unique but have fixed visuals)
      • SewegeCollection(building, pipes)(fixed visuals and not customisable)
      • Parks(small, large)(small and large parks differ in visuals, but cannot be customised)
      • Hospital(small, large)(small and large hospitals differ in visuals, but cannot be customised)
      • Busstops(static visuals)
      • Commercial(small, medium, large)(small, medium and large differ in visuals, all of them can have different colors)
    • People
      • (made with cubes, different colors, no clothes, minecraft like, health)
    • Homes
      • (inside of homes are hidden, rent depends on the landvalue, the surroundings, the pollution. small, medium and large homes have different rents and vary in parkingspace area and consume more/less power)
    • Factories
      • (insides are hidden, generate smoke visuals if possible. pollution depends on the size and number of work hours. salary depends on the educationlevel, number of work hours. trucks have parkingspace, power consumption depends on size and number of work hours. Only qualified people can work in the factory)
    • Garbage
      • (landfill has some parkingspace for 2-3 trucks, deploys trucks regularly. can get filled 100 percent. landfill dosent generate eletricity. It makes the surrounding less clean. refeneries generate-consume electricity, makes the surrounding polluted. It needs people)
    • PowerGenerators
      • (windmill farm, coal-power. power transportation requires physical wiring, cause sound and air pollution. Electricity generated is in megawatts)
    • Sewege Collection
      • (needs physical pipe wiring. consumes power, creates ground pollution, needs people)
    • Water Supply
      • (needs physical pipe wiring, consumes power)
    • Parks
      • (has some open space for people to walk around, keeps people happy, makes the surrounding less polluted, is fixed in visuals and not customisable)
    • Hospital
      • (fixed in visuals, can have 2-4 ambulances, restores health, consumes power, makes people happy)
    • Commercial
      • (consumes power, transportation of goods from factories, generates money, needs people, makes people happy, people buy goods)
    • Roads
      • (Only two lanes(one and two way), road layout mechanism yet to be decided)
    • TrafficLights
      • (colors: red, green, yellow. Fixed visuals, placement mechanism yet to be decided)
    • Simulation
      • movement of vehicles
      • movement of people(walk, cars, busses)
      • route planning
      • pollution(landvalue, airquality)
      • employment(jobs, salary, satisfaction)
      • todo_queue for all the entities

  • High-level access patterns

    • rendering the static entities(buildings, roads, signs)
    • rendering of dynamic entities(people, vehicles, particle effects)
    • movement of vehicles and route planning
    • updation of income, expenses, pollution, land_value
    • updation of the todo_queue
    • updation of flow of resources through the city
    • some particle systems maybe

  • Things to remember

    • Try to do it with just C-11, instead of C++.
    • (Build the system around / optimize) for the common case.
    • Don't update() and render() seperately.
    • Try to have linear / predictable access patterns.
    • Don't encapsulate the variables.
    • Don't rush and create a mess.
    • Be constant speed, don't start out fast and then slow down and stop.
